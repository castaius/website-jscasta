About JSCASTA

A magician who studies attention, emotion, and wonder.

JSCASTA is a professional magician, musician, psychologist, and creative experience designer. His work combines sleight of hand, storytelling, humor, psychology, music, and virtual production to create experiences that feel impossible, personal, and memorable.

  • Member of the Magic Castle in Hollywood
  • Graduate of Chavez Studio of Magic in Los Angeles
  • International performer across Latin America, North America, Europe, and Australia
  • Experience with global brands and corporate audiences
  • Bilingual performer in English and Spanish
  • Specialist in virtual and hybrid presentation experiences
