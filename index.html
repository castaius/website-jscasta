FAQ

A few practical details.

Where does strolling magic fit in the event schedule?

It works especially well during arrivals, pre-dinner drinks, networking sessions, cocktail receptions, and transitions. JSCASTA will recommend the timing after reviewing your run sheet.

Will guests be put on the spot?

Participation is invitational and respectful. JSCASTA approaches groups naturally and adjusts to the interest and tone of each audience.

Does the venue need to provide any equipment?

The core strolling format is self-contained and normally requires no stage, sound system, or dedicated lighting.

Can it work for a large guest list?

Strolling magic is performed for small groups throughout the room. The recommended performance length depends on the guest count, room layout, and how people will move through the event.

What happens if the schedule changes?

JSCASTA coordinates with the organiser and adjusts around speeches, service, awards, and other live changes where practical.
